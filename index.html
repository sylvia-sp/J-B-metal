About J&B Metal

J&B Metal is a leading custom sheet metal fabrication company serving Massachusetts and the surrounding areas. With years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality metalwork that meets the exacting standards of our clients.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with modern machinery and staffed by skilled craftsmen who are dedicated to bringing your vision to life. Whether you need a single prototype or large-scale production runs, we have the expertise and capacity to handle projects of any size.

From initial design consultation to final finishing touches, we work closely with our clients to ensure complete satisfaction with every project.
